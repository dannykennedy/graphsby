Suzanne is a writer, and a retired adult educator, counselor and ordained minister who lives and practices a "spiritually earthy" psycho-spirituality. Suzanne draws deeply from Jungian Psychology, Creation Spirituality, Christian Mysticism and teachings from Aboriginal cultures around the world in her work. She has authored seven books about her experience of a renewed earth-based culture and published numerous articles in Canadian and American magazines and grass-roots newsletters which focus on transpersonal psychology and a renewed earth centered awareness. She is married with seven children and thirteen grandchildren. She lives in Owen Sound, Ontario with her husband Rick.
